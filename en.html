Privacy Policy

Unitan

1. Introduction

toridyuessas ("Developer") is committed to protecting user privacy in the mobile application "Unitan" ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled within the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App does NOT collect the following personal information:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Address, location data
  • Account information (the App does not have login functionality)
  • Access to photos or contacts

Information stored on your device:

The App stores the following learning data only on your device. This data is not transmitted to external servers, and the Developer does not have access to it.

  • Created vocabulary cards (words, meanings, example sentences, etc.)
  • Wordbook information
  • Learning progress (mastery scores, review history)
  • Collections obtained through gacha
  • App settings

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. These services handle information according to their respective privacy policies.

Google AdMob (Advertising)

The App uses Google AdMob to deliver advertisements. AdMob may collect information such as device identifiers and advertising IDs for ad personalization and effectiveness measurement. For more details, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

OpenAI API (AI Features)

The App uses the OpenAI API to generate vocabulary cards and provide English spelling suggestions. When using these features, the entered word or phrase (up to 100 characters) is sent to the OpenAI API. No personally identifiable information is transmitted. For more details, please see OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Management

All learning data in the App is stored locally on your device. There is no cloud synchronization feature, and data is never transferred outside your device.

Uninstalling the App will delete all local data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The App does not require account registration and does not request personal information input.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

The Developer may update this Privacy Policy as needed. If changes are made, the new Privacy Policy will be posted on this page. We recommend checking this page periodically.
